MTN BetaGist

Talk for 6 mins | Get 3 mins extra

MTN BetaGist is a prepaid plan that lets you call anyone on any network in Nigeria at a flat rate of 30 kobo per second.

There’s more! For every 6 minutes of calls you make, you get back 3 minutes to keep the conversation going.

How to get on BetaGist

New customers? You’re already on it! MTN BetaGist is the default tariff plan for all new SIM registrations.

Existing MTN customer? No worries. You can switch to BetaGist for FREE using any of the channels below:

Text BG to 312
Dial *123*1*2#
Chat with Zigi

Frequently Asked Questions

A. MTN BetaGist is a prepaid tariff plan that allows customers to enjoy a FLAT rate of 30 kobo per second for all national calls across all local networks.

A. All MTN Prepaid customers are eligible to migrate to the MTN BetaGist plan.

A. By migrating to MTN BetaGist, you will enjoy: Flat call rate of 30 kobo per second for all national calls. You will enjoy a promotional offer of 3 minutes for every 6 minutes of outgoing local calls.

A. MTN Beta BetaGist is the default tariff plan for new customers after SIM registration.

However, existing customers can migrate to MTN BetaGist using any of the following channels:

Text BG to 312
Dial *123*2*1# or *405#
MTN ChatBot (Zigi):
Login to ChatBot (Zigi).
Select ‘Start a new conversation’.
Enter your phone number.
Enter the OTP.
Select ‘Services’.
Select ‘Tariff plan’.
Select ‘Personal’.
Select ‘BetaGist’.
Confirm the migration to BetaGist.
myMTN app:
Login to myMTN NG app.
Select the tariff name
On the “Tariff plans’ page, click the personal plan’ button.
Scroll down to select ‘BetaGist’.

A. Migration into MTN BetaGist is FREE if it is your first migration in 30 days. However, subsequent migrations within the same month will attract a migration fee of N150.
